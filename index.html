this system offers focusing more on your content and less on the css and html code that creates the design.

  • just choose a design you like, and add content to it.
  • if you dont like it, just switch between the designs to see how your content looks in different ones...
  • copy the code of the design you like best, and embed it on your webpage.
  • wish to create your own design? you're most welcome! your designs would automatically be shared with the rest of us.

why get a premium account?

  • if you wish to save your data online for quick reuse.
  • if you wish to create private designs that no one else might use.
